Welcome to The Green Beauty Community. The home of sustainable beauty advocates.

Clean Beauty

Clean Beauty focuses on products free from toxic or harmful ingredients. It emphasizes safety, thorough testing, and transparency in its ingredients, prioritizing human health.

Green Beauty

Green Beauty extends the principles of Clean Beauty to include environmental considerations. These products are non-toxic and designed to minimize environmental impact, often using biodegradable ingredients and eco-friendly packaging.

Sustainable Beauty

Sustainable Beauty considers the entire lifecycle of a product, from sourcing to disposal. It aims to meet current beauty needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet theirs, promoting long-term environmental stewardship.

About Us

Support The Community


The GlowFeba Community Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing sustainability in the beauty industry. We empower professionals and consumers through education, community building, and advocacy, driving positive change for a cleaner, greener future in beauty. Your support helps us continue our mission of making sustainable beauty the new standard.

Join the GlowFeba Community Foundation in our mission to transform the beauty industry: Follow us on social media to stay informed, volunteer your skills to make a direct impact, or donate to fuel our programs. Whether you're a beauty professional, enthusiast, or sustainability advocate, your support helps us create a greener, cleaner future for beauty.

Together, we can make sustainable beauty the new standard.
